ABOUT

DrommHuis products are original Indonesian products inspired by Indonesia, designed by young Indonesian designers, and made in Indonesia, using only materials from Indonesia. Through DrommHuis, we want to promote Indonesia's charm in cultural diversity. As a brand, we believe that Indonesian culture has been fundamentally shaped by history that has been engraved for centuries. We bring DrommHuis here to enable the younger generation to reconnect and appreciate the richness of Indonesia. Made from carefully selected materials and the finest craftsmanship from Indonesia, we present to you our collection and introduce Indonesia to the world. Each collection tells a different part of Indonesia and is a tribute to Indonesia's centuries old cultural heritage
